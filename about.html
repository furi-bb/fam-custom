About Fam

Clan Fam was founded in 2021 in response to a handful of friends deciding the clan they were a part of was getting to be too tolerant of hate speech, transphobia, and racism. Founders of Clan Fam believe women, queer folks, trans folks, and people of all sorts ethnicities and backgrounds deserve to be treated with dignity and a baseline of respect. We strive to create a cozy community worthy of our members' trust in us.
